About Contract Nerd

How It Works

The process is simple: first, you upload a PDF version of your contract to the Contract Nerd tool. ContractNerd will analyze key clauses and flag either illegal clauses which you should definitely reword or legal but potentially open to dispute. ContractNerd will both find problems and suggest solutions.

With this tool, we aim to make it easier for you to understand the potential risks involved in any contract before you sign it.

*ContractNerd is a research tool and is not intended to substitute for professional legal advice. We recommend seeking professional legal advice before signing an agreement.
